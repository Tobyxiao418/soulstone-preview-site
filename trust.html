FAQ draft

Questions the site must answer before formal launch.

These answers are intentionally conservative until real product operations are confirmed.

Are the stones natural?

The brand direction is natural crystals and freshwater pearls, but final supplier/material standards need confirmation before official sales.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping and production timing are not finalized in this MVP preview. A live site should show production time, shipping region and tracking policy clearly.

Can the exact colors vary?

Yes. Natural stones and pearls vary in color, texture and inclusions. The preview communicates the design direction, not a guarantee of identical stone pattern.

Does SoulStone make healing claims?

No. The intention and stone meanings are symbolic and personal. The product should not claim medical, therapeutic or financial benefits.

What must be decided before launch?

Pricing, inventory boundaries, payment provider, production time, shipping regions, returns, customer support and final packaging.
