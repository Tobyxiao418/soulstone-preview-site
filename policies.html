Shopify launch readiness

Materials, FAQ, shipping and returns — written before we open checkout.

This page is a pre-launch policy draft for team review. It is intentionally conservative so SoulStone does not over-promise before production, payment and fulfillment are finalized.

Materials

Natural materials vary — that is part of the product.

  • Natural crystals may vary in tone, inclusions, translucency and shape.
  • Freshwater pearls are organic and may not be perfectly round.
  • Silver hardware and cord colors must be confirmed against available stock before production.
  • Large irregular stones may change final bead count and bracelet fit.

Launch rule: every Shopify product needs a confirmed BOM before direct checkout is enabled.

Custom requests

Custom designs are reviewed before payment.

  • Customers can submit a design, wrist size, contact details and notes.
  • The team reviews material availability, feasibility, final price and production timeline.
  • Only after confirmation should the customer receive a payment link or Shopify draft order.
  • Fully automatic custom checkout should wait until production rules are proven.

Shipping draft

Made-to-order timing should be clear before purchase.

  • Fixed presets: estimated 5–10 business days before shipping, depending on product.
  • Custom requests: timeline confirmed manually before payment.
  • Tracking should be sent once the order ships.
  • International shipping should not be promised until carrier, cost and tax handling are confirmed.

Returns draft

Custom jewelry needs a stricter return rule.

  • Defective or damaged items should be handled with replacement, repair or refund review.
  • Made-to-order/custom pieces may not be eligible for general returns once production begins.
  • Size adjustment policy must be decided before formal launch.
  • Order cancellation window should be clearly stated, ideally before production starts.

FAQ draft

Questions customers will ask first.

Is this a real checkout yet?

Current preview is a prototype. Formal Shopify checkout opens only after product, payment and fulfillment are confirmed.

Can I fully customize my bracelet?

Yes, but the first live version should treat custom designs as requests. The team confirms price and feasibility before payment.

Do crystal meanings guarantee results?

No. SoulStone is symbolic jewelry for personal inspiration and gifting. It does not claim medical, therapeutic, financial or guaranteed outcomes.

Will the finished piece look exactly like the preview?

The design direction and material mix should match, but natural stones and pearls vary. Final photos and production notes should explain this clearly.

Before opening checkout

Minimum launch checklist.

  • Confirm 3 fixed preset BOMs.
  • Confirm final prices and production time.
  • Confirm packaging and story card format.
  • Prepare shipping, returns, privacy and terms pages.
  • Connect Shopify payments only after account and business details are ready.
  • Run at least one test order before public launch.
